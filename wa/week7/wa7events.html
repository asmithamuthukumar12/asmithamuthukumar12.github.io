Our Events!

Welcome to the CU SASA Events Page! Here we have information about our biggest events we host every year!

Garba (Fall)

Garba is a festival celebrating a traditional dance from Gujurat, India, performed in circles during the Hindu festival of Navratri to honor the goddess Durga. People gather in rounds, performing rhythmic steps, hand clapping, and spinning. Garba dance symbolizes the cyclical nature of life and the energy within all beings.

Diwali (Fall)

Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, marks the beginning of the fiscal year in India, and is also popularly celebrated by Sikhs, Jains, and some Buddhists. It is celebrated differently in each culture, but generally involves lighting oil lamps and symbolic victory over evil.

SASA Show (Spring)

SASA Show is an event hosted at CU every year by SASA, involving cultural talent groups both from and not from CU to celebrate talent and culture of all kinds. The night of dances is followed by food and socialization.
