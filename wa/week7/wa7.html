About SASA!

Welcome to the CU SASA Homepage!

SASA is a multicultural club that focuses on teaching both South Asians and non-South Asians about South Asian cultures and traditions, while also giving people a space to be themselves and hang out with their friends!

Who We Represent!

  • India
  • Maldives
  • Pakistan
  • Bhutan
  • Bangladesh
  • Nepal
  • Sri Lanka
  • Afghanistan
